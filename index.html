ABOUT

Foodly is a recipe inventor agency. Developing a recipe can be a well choreographed dance between research, testing and tasting. Other times it can feel as though there's no possible way to create the desired end goal.


Doing something completely new is incredibly difficult at this point. Doing something in a new way is much more likely to happen. Cooks have been clever and innovative for as long as there has been cooking. New equipment, new ingredients, new knowledge can all lead to new ways of doing things, and it's always possible to do new variations on a theme.
